Venmo blocking you from making transactions online?

Want to make transactions from their website anyway?


Instructions

  1. Drag this button into your bookmarks bar: Load Venmo Txn UI
  2. Login to Venmo.com and click the bookmark to load the transaction interface.
  3. Fill out the form and click Pay or Charge to pay or charge someone. Note: only one recipient per transaction is currently supported by this bookmarklet.

Notes

I have tested both paying and charging people with this bookmarklet and it worked for me. That being said, you're playing with real money here, so use at your own risk. No lifeguard on duty.
